Welcome to WireSocket. This guide will walk you through the process of setting up your account and preparing your first application for real-time synchronization.

---

## 1. Create a Tenant Account

Every organization starts by creating a Tenant account. This serves as the administrative hub for your collaborative ecosystem.

- Go to [dashboard.wiresocket.com](https://dashboard.wiresocket.com).
- Sign up using your email or GitHub account.
- **Initial State**: Your account will start in a "Pending" state while we prepare your dedicated cryptographic environment.

---

## 2. Account Approval & Activation

To ensure security and proper resource allocation, each new Tenant requires a one-time manual approval from the WireSocket administration.

- **Verification**: Our team reviews your registration.
- **Provisioning**: Upon approval, the system generates your unique **Asymmetric Signing Keys** and creates your billing Profile in our payment system (Dodo Payments).
- You will receive an email once your account is **Active**.

---

## 3. Set Up Your First App

An App is your gateway to the Data Plane. It contains the configuration and credentials your backend will use to authenticate users.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Application">
    Click on **Apps** in the sidebar and select **Add Application**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Region & Data Residency">
    Provide a name (e.g., `production-editor`) and select your **License
    Region**. WireSocket will automatically provision a regional database to
    store your app's metadata.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture Credentials">
    Once created, you will see your `client_id` and `client_secret`. **Copy them
    immediately.**
    <Warning>
      The `client_secret` is only shown once. If lost, you must regenerate it,
      which invalidates any service currently using the old secret.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## 4. Activate a Plan

Even for development, your App needs an active Plan to communicate with the Data Plane nodes.

- Navigate to your App's **Settings** or **Plans** tab.
- Choose a plan. The **Free** plan is perfect for testing (limit: one free plan per tenant).
- Click **Activate**. Your App is now ready to issue tokens.

<Info>
  Once active, the Control Plane pushes your configuration (allowed domains, OPM
  limits) to our global Data Plane nodes within seconds.
</Info>
